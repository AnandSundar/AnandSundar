As an **Agentic AI Security Engineer**, I specialize in securing **Multi-Agent and LLM systems** against prompt injection, model poisoning, data exfiltration, and adversarial attacks. I apply **OWASP Top 10 for LLMs**, enforce **IAM, RBAC, OPA/Rego**, and manage secrets and key governance to ensure secure, compliant AI/ML pipelines.

I drive **security automation**, vulnerability lifecycle management, GRC audits, and incident response — leveraging standards including **NIST AI RMF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, FedRAMP, and CMMC**.

As a **Software & GRC Engineer**, I use **AWS, Azure, Python, Go, PowerShell, and Bash** to automate compliance reporting, risk assessments, and remediation workflows — reducing attack surface and strengthening enterprise-grade defenses at scale.
